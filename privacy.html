4. How We Share Information

5. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards (encryption in transit, access controls, least-privilege practices). While we work to protect your data, no method is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We keep information only as long as necessary for scheduling operations, legal, or compliance purposes. Upon request or termination, we can delete or de-identify data, subject to any required retention.
